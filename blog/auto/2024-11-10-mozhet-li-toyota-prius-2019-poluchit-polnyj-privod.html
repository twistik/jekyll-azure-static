can toyota prius 2019 get all-wheel drive?

  • can toyota prius 2019 get all-wheel drive?

With its combined fuel economy rating, the current Toyota Prius remains one of the most efficient automobilesmobiles you can buy. Unfortunately for Toyota, the economy isn't selling automobiles like it used to. Prius sales are down 22 per cent for the year and are nowhere near what they were in 2007 and 2008. But can an all-wheel-drive version reverse that trend?

We can't really know what Toyota has in store for the Prius until the Los Angeles automobiles show begins this month. But based on the teaser Toyota released today, it's hard to imagine what else it might hint at.

The image clearly shows the Prius driving down a snow-covered road, which would be enough to start an all-wheel-drive rumour on its own. But the release also includes the words, ‘Fall is here, winter is near, but the 2019 Prius performs on the roads, whether snowy or clear.’

It's theoretically possible that Toyota plans to introduce a factory winter option to improve driving performance in snow, but it's not the kind of change you'd normally tease in a press release before an automobile salon.

Instead, we're sticking with the all-wheel drive theory, since it makes sense. Of course, it's also possible that Toyota has other changes in shop for the 2019 Prius. If so, let's hope one of them is a new front end with a less polarised look.
