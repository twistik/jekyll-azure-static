For Japan only, the Subaru WRX STI TC 380 produces 380bhp.

The TC 380 will only be sold in Japan

It's called the WRX STI TC 380, and as the name suggests, it makes 380bhp. If you do the maths, that's a bit more than a 25 per cent increase in power. Subaru says the TC 380 was developed in collaboration with World Rally Championship driver Toshihiro Arai. HKS supplies most of the power system upgrades, such as a new turbocharger. In addition to the power boost, which is nothing to sneeze at, the TC 380 gets a front strut, carbon fibre spoiler and TC 380 sports seats.

Only 50 of these limited edition STIs will be sold, each with a base price of 4,965,840. At current conversion rates, that comes out to about $44,000, but it's not a big deal. The TC 380 will only be sold in Japan! We don't even have a picture of the automobilesmobile to show, much less a chance of ever seeing one sold here.

If you live in Japan, Subaru says it's already taking orders for the WRX STI TC 380. Considering how few will be built, you better hurry or Subaru may run out. :)
