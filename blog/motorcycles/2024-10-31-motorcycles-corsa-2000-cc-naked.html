A UK motorcycles dealer called motorcycles Corsa is developing a 2000 cc racing motorcycle called the motorcycles Corsa 2K. The dealership has joined forces with T3 Racing, a manufacturer that develops tyres for road racing. The result is the creation of the motorcycles Corsa 2K, a naked street motorcycle with a unique design and high quality components boasting a huge 2000 cc v-twin engine, actually a 1961cc V1 Twin engine developed by S&S, which is brimming with 100bhp and a maximum torque of 183Nm and mated to a five-speed gearbox.

The Corsa 2K motorcycles was developed by T3 Racing, and the latest prototype photos show that this is a really impressive machine. The big S&S v-twin is typically used in larger cruisers, and this is probably the first time such a motorcyclescycle will get the same large displacement engine. The running gear is also of high quality, with Dymag carbon wheels, Ohlins suspension and Beringer brakes. The prototype contains many body panels that are now made of plastic, and once production starts these will be replaced with actual carbon fibre parts and panels.

Reportedly, despite the specifications and modern design, the Corsa 2K motorcycles is an air-cooled engine, although additional cooling methods are reportedly still being developed to keep the engine warm from the massive v-twin. Customers for this bike will be the discerning buyer looking for something exceptional, powerful and of course expensive. Only 20 motorcycles will be built, each priced at around $51,200.
