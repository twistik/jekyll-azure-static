2019 bmw x5 review

Facebook
Twitter
  • Main

  • 2019 bmw x5 review

The 2024 BMW X5 serves as the benchmark for the modern luxury crossover SUV. It impressively combines the attributes you'd normally associate with a BMW sedan and then adds true utility. A wide range of engine options and trim packages allow buyers to find the right configuration for their needs.

Engine options for the X5 include a turbocharged six-cylinder engine (300 horsepower, 300 lb-ft of torque), a diesel six-cylinder (255 hp, 413 lb-ft), a powerful V8 (445 hp, 480 lb-ft) and a hybrid hybrid (308 hp, 332 lb-ft). Regardless of what's under the bonnet, the X5 simultaneously delivers a comfortable ride and sporty handling, and the X5's all-wheel-drive system provides extra traction in wet weather or the occasional pavement disconnect

The X5 has a few downsides, most importantly interior space. It's not as easy as some rival crossovers with third row seats. Some of those rivals have fresher and newer designs. But overall, we think the X5 is a solid choice if you want a luxury automobilesmobile that offers a great combination of performance and utility.
