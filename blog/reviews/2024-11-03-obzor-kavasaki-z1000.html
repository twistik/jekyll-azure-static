The Kawasaki Z1000R Edition and Z1000R Performance keep the Z1000 firmly at the top of its class of big city motorcycles.

Front suspension with damping and preload adjustments to match the ZX636R are tuned for excellent feedback and the ability to cope with every riding style and situation. On the Kawasaki Z1000R, they are a perfect match for the new Ohlins rear shock absorber.

The new Brembo M50 radial mounted monobloc calipers match the radial pump master cylinder. Not only do these calipers look impressive, but better than anything money can buy in the world of motorcyclescycle manufacturing, the fact that they are manufactured from a single piece of metal ensures that there is no flex. They are mated to 330mm custom Brembo brake discs to provide huge stopping power, still giving remarkable feedback to the rider.

The Kawasaki Z1000R utilises the same 1043cc 4-cylinder engine of the standard Z1000. As a result, confident low and mid-range traction is available, with power increases all the way to the red line.

The induction sounds of this beast, like the whistle of engine revs, are simply infectious. A great reminder that the Z1000R really does have a wild side.

Alongside the Kawasaki Z1000, the front end of the motorcyclescle is characterised by a stunning submerged headlight shroud with its narrow state-of-the-art LED headlight.

The Z1000 version comes in its own very striking ‘R’ lyric, where accent design lines emphasise the aggressive stance of the Z1000R. Subtle colour variants in black and dark grey shades accentuate the sharp angular features

If the Kawasaki Z1000 has rewritten the rules in the supernova class, then the Z1000R has torn up the rule book

In the Kawasaki Z1000R Performance edition, you'll find Akrapovic Silencers.
